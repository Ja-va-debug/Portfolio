Who I Am

About Me

I’m a UI/UX designer with an academic background in computer science and foundational experience in front-end development, focused on creating intuitive and effective web and mobile application designs. Using tools such as Figma, Claude, and ChatGPT, I combine research-driven thinking with practical design methods to transform ideas into functional interfaces. My design process includes research, problem definition, ideation, wireframing, prototyping, and usability evaluation, allowing me to deliver efficient and well-structured designs that improve user experience and help clients build better digital products. Alongside my technical background, I also have experience as an artist, which strengthens my understanding of visual composition, color, and creative expression. This artistic perspective helps me create interfaces that are not only functional but also visually engaging and thoughtfully designed. My front-end experience also helps me design interfaces that are practical to implement and clear for developers to translate into code.
